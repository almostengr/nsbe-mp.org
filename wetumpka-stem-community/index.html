Wetumpka, Alabama STEM Community

Welcome to the vibrant STEM community in Wetumpka, Alabama! NSBE Montgomery Professionals is proud to be a part of the thriving STEM ecosystem in this dynamic city. Through our programs, events, and partnerships, we are dedicated to advancing STEM education, promoting diversity and inclusion in STEM fields, and inspiring the next generation of innovators and leaders.

About Wetumpka, Alabama

Wetumpka, Alabama is a hub of innovation and opportunity, with a rich history of scientific discovery and technological advancement. Home to Fort Toulouse-Fort Jackson Park, the Wetumpka Impact Crater, and Bibb Graves Bridge, Wetumpka, Alabama offers a wealth of resources and opportunities for STEM enthusiasts of all ages.
