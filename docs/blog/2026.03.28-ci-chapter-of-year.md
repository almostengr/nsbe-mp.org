We are thrilled to share some incredible news with our members, partners, and the wider community.
At the 2026 Annual Convention, our chapter was officially recognized by the Region 3 Professionals as
the recipient of the Collegiate Initiative (CI) Program  of the Year!

This prestigious award is given to the chapter that demonstrates outstanding
commitment to the mission of NSBE through impactful programming, professional development, and community engagement.
What This Award Represents

Winning the CI Program of the Year isn't just about a trophy; it is a reflection of the collective hard work
our members put in every single day. Over the past year, our chapter has focused on:

* Technical Excellence: Hosting workshops that bridge the gap between classroom theory and industry application.
* Mentorship & Growth: Strengthening the pipeline between our collegiate members and the Region 3 Professionals.
* Community Impact: Ensuring that our initiatives reach beyond our campus to inspire the next generation of Black engineers.
