2008 National Society of Black Engineers – MAE Academic Scholarship Program
 

Each year the Montgomery Alumni Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE-MAE) awards $500 scholarship(s) to deserving minority high school seniors with the intent of pursuing a technical degree from an accredited college or university. Scholarship winners may use their awards at any qualifying institution and for any field of study they choose.
 
Evaluation Criteria*

Applicants will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Completed application postmarked by *New Deadline*: February 1, 2008

  • Academic achievement (GPA and/or SAT scores)

  • Personal essay describing career goals, extracurricular activities, relevant job experiences, community involvement, etc.

  • Membership in professional/academic organization(s)

 

*If selected as a finalist, you may be required to interview with the NSBE-MAE scholarship committee.

 

Application Instructions:

To ensure that your application is complete, provide all of the applicable information below:

  • Completed application *

  • Current official transcript of your grades from high school or all colleges you have attended

  • Two (2) letters of recommendation from teachers, employers, guidance counselors, academic advisors, and/or pastors  

  • Copy of SAT and/or ACT test scores (high school students only)

  • Personal Statement (500 words or less)

 

*NOTE:  All applications should be typed or legibly printed in black ink.

 

Mail your completed application and all attachments by *New Deadline* February 1, 2008 to:

NSBE-MAE Scholarship Program

Attn: Scholarship Program Administrators

P. O. Box 210176

Montgomery, Alabama 36121

 

If you have questions, please call (334)-318-0728
