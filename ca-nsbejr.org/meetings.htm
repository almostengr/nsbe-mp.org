What is Engineering?

How have engineers impacted our lives?  Learn the answers to these and plenty more questions during this presentation.  We will be discussing what is meant by "STEM" careers.  We will also show videos of fun science experiments, including the projects of the month.

Kid Zone and NSBE Jr (Middle):

Balloon Powered Race Cars The Challenge
Students investigate Newton's Third Law of Motion by designing and constructing rocket-powered racing cars using styrofoam food trays, straws, balloons, tape, and other common household supplies.
