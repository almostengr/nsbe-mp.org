WOULD YOU LIKE TO JOIN CA-NSBE JR. ?

 

 

 

Membership Form
NSBE Jr. membership is annual and open to all students grades 6 -12.  Students interested in becoming an active member of the Central Alabama Area NSBE Jr. Chapter should first complete the NSBE Jr. Membership Form:

CA-NSBE Jr. Membership Form

 

Students are asked to submit their NSBE Jr. Membership Forms to:

 

NSBE-MAE
PO Box 210176
Montgomery, AL 36121

The fee to participate as a member of NSBE Jr. is $10.00/Student (yearly). Membership fees can be submitted to the address above with the Membership Form or submitted in person during the next NSBE Jr. Meeting. Key benefits of joining the Central Alabama Area NSBE Jr. Chapter include:

 

  • FREE Bi-annual subscription to the NSBE's magazine, The Bridge

  • Participation in the Discover "E" Lecture Series

  • SAT/ACT and College Admissions Preparation

  • Scholarship and Awards

  • Try-Math-A-Lon

  • Professional Development

  • NSBE Web Activities

  • PCI Regional and National Conferences
