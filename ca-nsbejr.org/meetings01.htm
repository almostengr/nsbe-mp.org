Engineering Discipline of the Month:

 

Mechanical Engineering

 

Speaker: James and Jessie Dixon

 

 

"Auto Motion"

 

 

Ever wonder what makes a car run?  Well this month is your chance.  We will focus on "auto motion." Learn one of the many facets of mechanical engineering by designing a rubber band car, a mousetrap car, a solar powered car, and many others.  We will look at the beginning, "Autocad", which shows where a car design starts.  Spend some time this month checking out mechanical engineering.  We will be testing your skills!!!!!
